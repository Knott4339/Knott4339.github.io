Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Jones

This portrait of Madmosielle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the french Neoclassical artist Jean August Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvere. It is the third of these portraits in the Rivière family artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium : Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 X 70 cm
